may 10

As per my April 28 blog entry, I worked on making a scheduler app for high schoolers and college students. This was inspired by my sister who had often asked me to help her figure out her gpa and by the current coronavirus pandemic's effect on the college system. Many colleges have changed their grading system and it can become hard to understand the effect a P/NP grade will have on your GPA, a concern for many students entering a receeding job market. I also made a Flask Application that helps people use the Spotify API to find songs that match keys and tempo to help with mixes.
